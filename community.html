Worth Noticing

Sometimes what changes us
isn't a grand revelation.

Every community has people who quietly shape how it feels to live there. Some grow food. Some preserve traditions. Some create beauty. Some build places where others gather.

Worth Noticing follows the many ways people participate in the life of a place, noticing how they help it continue.

This is a living record of who is already helping this place flourish — gathered by someone who believes that paying attention is one of the quiet ways we learn to participate, too.
